Data science is the science of understanding and mining data. It is an interdisciplinary field that requires an understanding of statistics, computer science, and mathematics, along with domain field knowledge. In this course, students will be instructed on all areas, ranging from introduction to basic EDA to designing their own Artificial Neural Networks. At the end of this course, students will be able to make their own models for supervised and unsupervised learning tasks and reach a competitive level of accuracy.
The pre-requisites for this course are Python, and mathematics. Python is mandatory as all hands-on sessions will be in Python.
